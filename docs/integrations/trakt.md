# Trakt Integration

Trakt can keep your watch history, progress, and personal lists synchronized across supported Nuvio devices.

## Connect Trakt

1. Open Nuvio and go to **Settings**.
2. Select **Trakt** or **Integrations**, depending on your platform.
3. Choose **Connect Trakt**.
4. Follow the device authorization instructions shown by Nuvio.
5. Return to Nuvio after approval and confirm that your account is connected.

::: tip Separate profiles
If you use multiple Nuvio profiles, each profile can connect to a different Trakt account.
:::

## What syncs

- Watch history
- Playback progress
- Watchlists and supported personal lists
- Up-next and continue-watching data where supported

## Troubleshooting

If progress stops syncing:

1. Confirm the device is online.
2. Open the Trakt integration page and check that the account is still connected.
3. Disconnect and reconnect Trakt to refresh authorization.
4. Restart Nuvio and allow a few moments for the first synchronization.

For broader playback or account problems, see [Troubleshooting](../troubleshooting.md).
