# Nuvio Wiki: Cross-Platform UI & Customization Guide

<a id="top"></a>

> **Note on platform tags:** Throughout this guide, features tagged **[Mobile Only]** or **[TV Only]** are exclusive to that platform. Any feature listed without a tag is available on both the Mobile and TV apps.

Whether you are swiping through catalogs on a phone or navigating with a remote on the couch, Nuvio offers deep customization to tailor your streaming environment. This guide breaks down how to configure visual elements, organize your media, and adjust playback navigation across both the Mobile and TV applications.

---

## 1. Themes & Appearance

Both platforms share a unified color palette but offer device-specific methods to handle deep blacks and typography.

* **Accent Colors:** Select from White, Crimson, Ocean, Violet, Emerald, Amber, or Rose to highlight active menus, buttons, and playback progress bars.
* **OLED/AMOLED Settings:**
  * **[Mobile Only]** A straightforward *AMOLED Black* toggle enables pure black backgrounds to save battery and reduce eye strain.
  * **[TV Only]** Granular control is available — enable *AMOLED Mode* for the background layer, and separately toggle *Pure Black Surfaces* for interactive elements like cards, panels, and containers.
* **[TV Only] Typography:** Adjust the *App Font* (e.g., Inter) to optimize legibility for large-screen reading.

## 2. Home Screen & Layout Structures

The catalog interaction experience is fundamentally tailored to touch versus remote controls.

* **[TV Only] Layout Styles:** Choose between a *Modern View*, *Grid View*, or *Classic View*. In the Modern view, you can toggle *Landscape Posters* and enable a *Fullscreen Hero Backdrop* for a highly visual interface.
* **[Mobile Only] Hero Sections:** Use the *Show Hero Section* toggle to display an interactive carousel at the top of the home screen, driven directly by your selected Hero Catalogs.
* **[TV Only] Navigation & Sidebar:** Enable a floating *Modern Sidebar* and opt to *Collapse Sidebar* by default to maximize poster screen real estate.
* **Catalog Management:** Both apps allow you to hide unreleased content to keep your library clean.
  * **[Mobile Only]** Deep organizational control to lock, hide, or pin specific collections (like *Discover*, *Franchises*, or *Streaming Services*).
  * **[TV Only]** A *Show Catalog Type* option displays Movie/Series suffixes next to titles.

## 3. Detail Page & Metadata Formatting

Fine-tune how movie and show landing pages are structured and presented.

* **Backgrounds & Artwork:**
  * **[Mobile Only]** Enable *Cinematic Background* for blurred screen backdrops.
  * **[TV Only]** Toggle *Prefer meta from external addon* to dictate where your metadata originates.
* **[Mobile Only] Episodes Layout:** Choose between *Horizontal* (backdrop-style row cards) or *List* (detail-first stacked cards) when viewing seasons.
* **Trailers:**
  * **[Mobile Only]** Toggle *Hero Trailer Playback* to view previews directly within the top metadata hero.
  * **[TV Only]** Manage *Auto-play Trailers* to start previews after a period of inactivity, or toggle a dedicated *Show Trailer Button* if you prefer manual control.
* **[TV Only] Data Formatting:** Toggle the *Show full release date* option to see the exact premiere day instead of just the release year.
* **[Mobile Only] Section Visibility:** Granularly hide or reorder modular detail page sections including Actions, Overview, Production, Cast, Comments, Trailers, and Episodes.

## 4. "Continue Watching" & Up Next Behavior

Both apps provide tools to streamline binge-watching flows and prevent spoilers.

* **[Mobile Only] Poster Card Style:** Customize the "Continue Watching" shelf by switching between TV-style *Card*, info-dense *Wide*, or artwork-first *Poster* styles.
* **Episode Tracking:** Enable *Up Next From Furthest Episode* to ensure the app surfaces the latest unwatched episode (disable this if you are actively rewatching an old season).
* **Spoiler Prevention:** Use *Blur Unwatched Episodes* (Detail Page) and *Blur Unwatched in Continue Watching* so thumbnail images do not reveal major plot points.
* **Thumbnail Preferences:** Toggle *Prefer/Use Episode Thumbnails* to prioritize specific episode stills over the generic series backdrop.
* **Sort Order:** Arrange your "Continue Watching" queue by *Default* (based on recency) or *Streaming Style* (released items prioritize first, with upcoming unreleased episodes pushed to the end).

### Platform Comparison Quick Reference

| Feature | Mobile App | TV App |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Pure Black Surfaces** | AMOLED Black (Global toggle) | Granular (Backgrounds vs. Panels) |
| **Home Layout Views** | Standard vertical scrolling rows | Selectable Modern, Grid, or Classic Views |
| **Sidebar Navigation** | Bottom/Hamburger tab menu | Floating Modern Sidebar (Collapsible) |
| **Detail Page Layout** | Reorderable UI sections & tabs | External Metadata toggles & Auto-Play trailers |
| **Continue Watching Cards** | Selectable Card, Wide, or Poster styles | Automatically adapts to chosen Home Layout View |
